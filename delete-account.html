What happens next

  • We’ll verify ownership via your account email or a one-time code.
  • Once verified, we’ll queue your account for deletion and confirm by email.
  • Typical processing time: 7–30 days depending on data backups and legal obligations.
  • Certain records may be retained if required by law (e.g., payments, fraud prevention).

If you deleted the app from your device, your account still exists until this process is complete.

FAQ

Is there an in‑app delete option?

Yes. Go to Profile → Settings → Account → Delete Account in the MGL Club app (latest version). If you cannot log in, use the email form above.

What data is deleted?

Profile, username, Moments, messages (where feasible), purchases & gifts history, and other account‑linked data in our systems. Data in other members’ content (e.g., quotes or replies) may be anonymized.

What is retained?

Records required by law (e.g., transaction records, anti‑fraud logs) may be retained for the minimum period required and are then deleted or anonymized.

How do I cancel a deletion request?

Reply to the confirmation email as soon as possible. If deletion has already started, we may not be able to restore your account.
